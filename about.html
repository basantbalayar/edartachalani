About e-Darta Chalani System

e-Darta Chalani System is a digital document registration and correspondence management platform designed specifically for Nepal.

Our system helps government offices, municipalities, universities, banks, and enterprises replace manual darta-chalani registers with a secure digital workflow.

Our Mission

To support transparent, efficient, and paperless governance through modern digital solutions.

Designed in Nepal

This system is designed by Basant Balayar with local administrative processes and compliance in mind.
