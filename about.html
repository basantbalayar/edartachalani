About e-Darta Chalani System

e-Darta Chalani System is a digital document registration and correspondence management platform designed specifically for Nepal. We help organizations streamline their document workflows and improve governance through modern technology.

Our system helps government offices, municipalities, universities, banks, and enterprises replace manual darta-chalani registers with a secure digital workflow.

Our Mission

To support transparent, efficient, and paperless governance through modern digital solutions in Nepal. We believe technology should empower organizations to work smarter, faster, and more securely.

Who We Serve

  • Government offices and departments
  • Municipalities and local bodies
  • Universities and educational institutions
  • Banks and financial institutions
  • Private enterprises and organizations

Designed in Nepal

This system is designed by Basant Balayar with deep understanding of local administrative processes and compliance requirements in Nepal. Every feature is built with Nepali governance in mind.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering a secure, user-friendly, and efficient platform that helps organizations:

  • Streamline document management workflows
  • Improve data security and compliance
  • Reduce operational costs
  • Enhance transparency and accountability
  • Enable paperless governance
