What is e-Darta Chalani System?

e-Darta Chalani System is a secure digital document registration and correspondence management platform designed for government offices, municipalities, universities, banks, and enterprises in Nepal.

Digital Darta Chalani Software for Nepal Government

This system replaces manual registers and paper-based workflows with a centralized, searchable, and audit-ready digital solution that aligns with Nepal government office procedures.

Key Features

  • Incoming & Outgoing Digital Darta–Chalani
  • Inter-Office Chalani Tracking
  • Role-Based Access Control
  • QR Code Verification
  • Audit Logs & Reports
  • Fiscal Year-Wise Records
  • Bilingual Support (Nepali & English)
