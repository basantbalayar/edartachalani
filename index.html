What is e-Darta Chalani System?

e-Darta Chalani System is a secure digital document registration and correspondence management platform designed for government offices, municipalities, universities, banks, and enterprises in Nepal.

Key Features

  • ✓ Incoming & Outgoing Digital Darta–Chalani
  • ✓ Inter-Office Chalani Tracking
  • ✓ Role-Based Access Control
  • ✓ QR Code Verification
  • ✓ Audit Logs & Reports
  • ✓ Bilingual Support (Nepali & English)

Digital Darta Chalani in Nepal

Digital Darta Chalani in Nepal helps government offices move away from paper-based registers to a secure, searchable, and centralized digital system. It improves transparency, reduces processing time, and ensures proper record keeping aligned with Nepali government procedures.
