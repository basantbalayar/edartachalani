My Story: From Branch Desk to Digital Solution

I didn't set out to build a software company. I built this system because I lived the problem every single day, and I couldn't accept it as "just how offices work in Nepal."

My Journey in Insurance

6.7 Years in Underwriting (Branch Level)

I spent most of my career at the branch desk, working directly with customers, policies, and documents. Every day, I handled physical files, darta registers, and chalani dispatches.

And every day, I witnessed the same problems:

  • Documents getting delayed without anyone knowing where they were
  • Files mysteriously going missing after dispatch
  • No clear record of who was responsible for what
  • Repeated audit questions that nobody could answer with proof
  • Internal conflicts because "I was never told" was a common defense

These weren't theoretical problems. They affected real work, real customers, and real compliance.

2 Years in Corporate IT

Then I moved to the Corporate Office IT department. This gave me a completely different perspective.

Now I could see the problem from the other side:

  • Management asking for data that didn't exist
  • Auditors questioning processes that had no documentation
  • Regulators requiring accountability that was impossible to prove
  • The branch teams unable to explain what happened to important documents
